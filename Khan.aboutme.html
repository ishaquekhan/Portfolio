At the current moment, I am a senior enrolled in Dublin High School, in Dublin, California. Ever since my early childhood, I have been thoroughly fascinated by computers and all they could do. After the first time I experienced using a computer, I have dreamed of becoming a software engineer, making programs and applications that people use every day of their lives. Because of this dream, I have made many steps toward achieving my ultimate goal. I took CSE (Computer Science and Software Engineering) during sophomore year and took AP Computer Science A during my junior year. These classes have helped me develop my foundation in the basics of computer science.

In order to achieve my goal of becoming a computer scientist, there are certain qualities I have developed throughout the pursuit of my dream. By far, the most important of these qualities have been sheer determination and perseverance. Those qualities are the reason I am still in pursuit of my objective. Without them, I would not have a reason to do what I do.
