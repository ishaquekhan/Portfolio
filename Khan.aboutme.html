Growing up in Silicon Valley, I was lucky to be exposed to the forefront of modern technology. This class helped me solidify my core foundation in the basics of computer science. In my junior year, I took AP Computer Science A to become familiar with the conceptual side of object-oriented programming and applied these skills in Java, particularly in the form of Android applications. I used the skills I learned when I developed my Discord bot.
